How to Measure
1

Use a String or Paper

Wrap a piece of string or paper around your finger at the widest point (usually the knuckle).

2

Mark the Measurement

Mark where the string/paper meets, then measure the length in millimeters.

3

Consider the Fit

The ring should slide on with slight resistance and come off with some effort. Consider going up half a size if between sizes.

4

Measure Multiple Times

Fingers can swell throughout the day. Measure a few times for accuracy, especially in the evening.

Ring sizing may vary slightly between manufacturers. Always confirm with a professional jeweler for important purchases.
